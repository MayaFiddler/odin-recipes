Italian Chicken Skillet

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • minced garlic
  • sliced cherry tomatoes
  • chopped spinach
  • chicken broth
  • heavy cream
  • grated parmesan cheese
  • diced shallots

How to make it

  1. Begin by seasoning 4 boneless, skinless chicken breast with garlic salt, pepper, and 1/2 tsp Italian seasoning.
  2. Using a large skillet melt 2 tbsp of butter over medium high heat and add the chicken breast. Cook 5 minutes a side until they begin to brown and juices run clear, remove from skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet melt an additional 2 tbsp of butter over medium heat and add minced garlic, diced shallots, and tomatoes. Saute, stirring often for 5-8 minutes until the tomatoes begin to soften.
  4. Stir in chicken stock, heavy cream, parmesan cheese, and 1/2 tsp Italian seasoning. Stir until cheese has melted. Add in the spinach and cook 1-2 minutes until spinach has cooked down and softened.
  5. Return chicken breast to skillet and spoon sauce over the top of each chicken breast. Garnish with parmesan cheese and serve.
